Educational Background & Interests

I am a graduate of Vassar College where I earned a Bachelor's in Science, Technology, and Society Studies (STS). I learned how to approach complex social problems that involve an understanding (or often, a misunderstanding) of science & technology. My senior thesis was about Net Neutrality debate and Internet governance. Other STS topics I continue to think about include accessibility to technology, politics and critical philosophy of technology, information vs knowledge, science vs religion, gender & science, military origins of technologies, and much more.
