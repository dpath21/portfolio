About Me

I'm Divya

Click through to learn more about me.

Data Analyst

I currently work full time as a healthcare finance data analyst. I work primarily with Excel. My previous data analysis role was for a consulting firm that provided financial advising for colleges and universities. I really enjoy working with data on both a micro and macro level: I like investigating changes in trends or totals by pinpointing specific data behind them, and equally like thinking about how to best structure data for versatile use and clear communication.

Web Developer

I began learning web development through ElevenFifty Academy in March of 2021. Through their program, I have learned HTML, CSS, and Javascript. The skills I've gained so far have created this very website as well as the projects you see linked in the boxes above. I still have much more to learn!

Educational Background & Interests

I'm a proud graduate of Vassar College, where I earned a Bachelor's in Science, Technology, and Society Studies. I learned how to approach complex social problems that involve a understanding (or misunderstanding) of science & technology. My senior thesis was about Net Neutrality debate, which explored the conflicts of interests between government, corporations, and end-users as they concern the ownership and monetization of the Internet. Examples of other topics I explored at Vassar and continue to think about include science education, information vs knowledge, knowledge systems, science vs religion, critical philosophy of technology, accessibility to tech, gender & science, healthcare policy, climate & energy policy, military roots of technologies, AI, the list goes on!

Everything Else

Some other loves of mine: volunteering at Global Gifts (a local, fair trade non-profit), playing guitar & making music, faith & spirituality, taking walks & exercising, keeping in touch with friends and family, chickpeas, strawberry-banana smoothies, painting, candles, stained glass lamps, art museums, supportive & sturdy shoes, and more!
